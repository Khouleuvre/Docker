Steven Universe Fan Site

Steven Universe is the main protagonist of the show. He is a young boy who inherited a gemstone from his mother, Rose Quartz. He is a member of the Crystal Gems, a team of magical beings who protect the Earth from evil. He is kind, optimistic, and adventurous.

Learn more about Steven on his official site.

Garnet is the leader of the Crystal Gems. She is a fusion of two gems, Ruby and Sapphire, who love each other very much. She is strong, wise, and mysterious. She has the power of future vision, which allows her to see possible outcomes of events.

Learn more about Garnet on her Wikipedia page.

Amethyst is the fun-loving and rebellious member of the Crystal Gems. She was created on Earth in a place called the Kindergarten, where gems were made by injecting the ground with gem essence. She is playful, energetic, and loyal. She can shape-shift into different forms and objects.

Learn more about Amethyst on her Wikipedia page.

Pearl is the elegant and graceful member of the Crystal Gems. She was one of Rose Quartz's closest followers and friends. She is smart, sophisticated, and perfectionist. She can summon a spear as her weapon and create holograms for various purposes.

Learn more about Pearl on her Wikipedia page.
